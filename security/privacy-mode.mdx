## What Privacy Mode Does

**When enabled, we guarantee that code data is never stored by our model providers or used for training.** Privacy Mode is included on all paid plans.

When Privacy Mode is on:

- **Zero data retention** with our model providers for prompts and code snippets you send during chats.
- **No training-data use.** We do not use your prompts, generated outputs, or code snippets to train our models, and we do not permit third parties to use them for training.
- **Same Summer Engine.** Privacy Mode does not change which models you can pick or how the engine works. It changes how your data is handled.

<Info>
Privacy Mode is a paid-plan feature. The Free plan does not include Privacy Mode.
</Info>

## What Happens When Privacy Mode Is Off

Privacy Mode is **off by default** for all accounts.

When Privacy Mode is not enabled, we may use and store prompts, editor actions, code snippets, and other code data and actions to improve our AI features and train our models. Free accounts do not have access to Privacy Mode and operate in this mode by default.

For the full legal text, see our [Privacy Policy](https://summerengine.com/privacy), specifically the section "How we use your prompts and code".

## How to Enable Privacy Mode

Privacy Mode is available in your account settings on any paid plan. Once enabled, it applies to all chats and operations sent from your account from that point forward.

## Who Sees Your Code

Even with Privacy Mode enabled, prompts and code snippets you send are transmitted to our model providers in order to generate responses. Privacy Mode controls **retention and training** by those providers and by us — it does not prevent the content from being processed in real time to answer you.

For a full list of subprocessors and infrastructure that may see code data, see our [Security Overview](/security/overview).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Privacy Mode on by default?**
No. Privacy Mode is off by default for all accounts. Pro users can enable it in account settings.

**Is Privacy Mode available on the Free plan?**
No. Privacy Mode is included on all paid plans. The Free plan does not include Privacy Mode.

**Does Privacy Mode change which AI models I can use?**
No. Privacy Mode changes how your data is handled, not which models are available.

**What about content I share as Feedback or in a bug report?**
Content you explicitly share with us as Feedback or in a bug report may be reviewed and used to improve Summer Engine, regardless of Privacy Mode. See our [Privacy Policy](https://summerengine.com/privacy) for details.
