## Console Requirements

Console publishing requires:

- **Registered company** or legal entity
- **Platform holder approval** (Nintendo, Sony, or Microsoft)
- **Dev kit hardware** ($450-$10,000+ per platform)
- **NDAs and agreements**

<Info>Build and validate your game on PC first. Most successful indies release on Steam/itch.io, then port to consoles once they have revenue and proven demand.</Info>
